Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a rich and comforting Italian classic made with layers of tender pasta, savory meat sauce, creamy ricotta, and melted cheese. Baked to golden perfection, each bite is a delicious combination of flavors and textures, making it the ultimate comfort food.

Ingredients

    For the Meat Sauce:

  • 500g ground beef (or a mix of beef and pork)
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (400g) crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • 1 tbsp olive oil

    • For the Ricotta Mixture:

  • 250g ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 50g grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

    • Other Ingredients:

  • 250g lasagna sheets (pre-cooked or fresh)
  • 200g mozzarella cheese, grated
  • 50g Parmesan cheese, grated

Directions

  1. Make the Meat Sauce

    • Heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat. Add onion and garlic, saut√© until soft.
    • Add the ground meat, cook until browned.
    • Stir in tomato paste, crushed tomatoes, oregano, basil, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 20 minutes.

  2. Prepare the Ricotta Mixture

    • In a bowl, mix ricotta, egg, Parmesan, salt, and pepper until smooth.

  3. Assemble the Lasagna

    • In a baking dish, spread a thin layer of meat sauce.
    • Add a layer of lasagna sheets, followed by a layer of ricotta mixture, then meat sauce, and mozzarella. Repeat layers until all ingredients are used, finishing with mozzarella and Parmesan on top.

  4. Bake

    • Preheat oven to 180¬∞C (350¬∞F).
    • Cover with foil and bake for 30 minutes. Remove foil and bake for another 10-15 minutes until golden and bubbly.

  5. Rest & Serve

    • Let it rest for 10 minutes before slicing.
